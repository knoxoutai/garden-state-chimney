Why Choose a Professional for Wood Stove Installation?

Wood stoves operate at high temperatures and vent real combustion byproducts. An improper installation can lead to house fires, carbon monoxide exposure, voided warranties, and failed inspections. Our NFI-certified team ensures correct clearances to combustibles, proper chimney or flue connection, and code-compliant hearth protection.

Our Wood Stove Installation Process

  • Consultation & sizing: We help you choose a stove sized correctly for the space you want to heat.
  • Site assessment: We evaluate clearances, flooring, and your existing chimney or venting options.
  • Professional installation: We set the stove, connect the venting, and protect the hearth area to code.
  • Safety check & walkthrough: We confirm proper draft and operation, then show you how to use and maintain your new stove.

Frequently Asked Questions

Can you install a wood stove in a home without a chimney?
Often, yes. Depending on your home, we can install the appropriate venting system. We'll assess your space and recommend the safest, code-compliant option during the consultation.
Do I need a permit for a wood stove installation?
Many New Jersey municipalities require a permit and inspection for a new wood stove. We install to code so your system passes inspection, and we can advise on local requirements.
How do I choose the right size wood stove?
Stove size should match the area you want to heat. An oversized stove runs inefficiently; an undersized one won't keep up. We'll help you select the right model for your space.
How much does wood stove installation cost?
It varies by stove model, venting needs, and hearth requirements. We don't list prices online, but we'll provide a detailed estimate. Call 973-519-0802 to discuss.
