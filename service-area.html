What Services Does Garden State Chimney Offer?

As a full-service chimney company, we do it all — not just sweeping, inspections, or simple repairs. We’re proud to offer:

  • Level 1 & 2 Chimney Inspections and Chimney Cleanings
  • Gas Fireplace Services
  • Leak Repairs: flashing repair, crown rebuilds, cap install/replacement, chase cover install/replacement, waterproofing
  • Masonry Repairs: repointing/tuckpointing, brick replacement, partial and full rebuilds
  • Fireplace Repairs: firebox repointing/tuckpointing, firebox rebuild, prefab refractory panel replacement
  • Relining: water heater/utility flue relining, HeatShield® resurfacing, stainless steel
  • Installations: wood stove installation, gas fireplace installation, gas log installation
  • Dryer Vent Cleaning

Why Are Certifications Important in the Chimney Industry?

The chimney industry isn’t well-regulated — anyone with the tools and a ladder can call themselves a chimney company. That’s why we ensure Garden State is properly licensed and insured, and that every sweep we hire has rigorous training through organizations like the CSIA.
