About the Owner

Meet Daniel Barisonek

Owner and operator Daniel Barisonek was born to a working-class family in Wayne, NJ. Growing up with five siblings — right in the middle of the pack — he learned patience, sharing, and caring early in life.

Working since he was 13 — shoveling driveways, mowing lawns, and carrying bags as a caddy — hard work and doing the right thing have been the basis of Daniel’s daily life. His 14 years in the chimney industry have given him exceptional knowledge of fireplace and chimney maintenance and repair.

For Daniel, doing the right thing means more than making a quick buck — and his business is operated accordingly.
